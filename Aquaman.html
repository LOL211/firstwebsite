Aquaman or Arthur Curry was left on land by his mother Queen of Atlantis to his father a lighthouse man. The atlanteans, seeing Arthur was half-human were against him and would kill him. He lived in Boston and grew up thinkng he was human. But when he grew up Atlantis showed up and took him back.

He then was offered the throne of Atlantis, being the descendant of the last king but he denied it and his Atlantean brother took it. In Atlantis he also met his soon-to-be wife Mera

Due to Atlantis attacking on the order of his brother because Atlantis was attacked he was forced to take the crown and became the king of Atlantis

He also helped with the Justice League and became one its founding memebers as well.
