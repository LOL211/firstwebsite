Diana Prince or Wonder Woman was the child of Zeus (King of the Gods) and Hippolyta (Queen of the Amazons). She was born of the Amazonian Island of Themiyscira.

She then grows up as an Amazonian Warrior, isolated from rest of the world. When a plane crashes she finds a person, Steve Trevor, she nurses him back to health and they both fall in love.

Soon her mother, Queen of the Amazons holds a contest to choose who shall help reuturn Steve Trevor and represent the Amazonian Mission of peace in the world and gain the title of Wonder Woman.

Diana wins this contest and goes with Steve Trevor to man's world as Wonder Woman and becomes a superhero. In one of her adventures she defeates Ares and takes his title.

She then helps found the Justice League and becomes one of its first 7 members
